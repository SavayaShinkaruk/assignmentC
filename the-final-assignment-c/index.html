THIS SNEAKER COLLECTION WILL INSPIRE YOUR STREETWEAR IN NEW WAYS

Savaya Shinkaruk is a sneaker enthusiast living in Toronto, Canada who owns a sneaker brand, and has partnered with Nike to create a new collection.

The world of fashion is something Savaya Shinkaruk has been apart of for only a few years. But she is making a name for herself in multiple ways. From working in fashion communications, to being a personal shopper, to being a fashion forward sneakerhead, to now becoming a sneaker designer with Nike.

Her inspiration came from wearing sneakers on a daily basis. She told us that she would only wear sneakers out. Whether it was running errands or to go out for a nice dinner, she styled her looks around her sneaker collection. Wearing her favourite brands such as Nike, Vans, Converse, and Golden Goose Deluxe Brand Runners she decided to come apart of the sneaker world. This is where we want to welcome her brand: SVEA + Nike.

SVEA + Nike is a brand you have to look into. The coolest feature of this partnership is you can custom make a limited edition sneaker with Savaya for any occassion! To date Savaya has released 10 sneakers that are available online at Nike, Net-a-porter, FarFetch and in-store at Nordstrom. She only makes a few sneakers per each design, so get them while you can!

Savaya Shinkaruk is one Canadian designer that is in on our radar, and should be on yours too. She has given us some secret informaiton that she is looking to expand her brand with other clothing designers. But this is just a fun secret to share for now.

Follow Savaya Shinkaruk on Instagram and go to her website to check out her designs and projects. And tag us in your social media pictures wearing your new SVEA + Nike sneakers @Coveteur.
